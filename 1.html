The RecyclerView widget is a more advanced and flexible version of ListView.
In the RecyclerView model, several different components work together to display your data.
The overall container for your user interface is a RecyclerView object that you add to your
layout. The RecyclerView fills itself with views provided by a layout manager that
you provide. You can use one of our standard layout managers (such as LinearLayoutManager
or GridLayoutManager), or implement your own.
